Complex Base

Complex Base is a striking, contemporary apartment building that rises gracefully into the skyline, a blend of elegance and modern design. The five-story structure hosts 20 thoughtfully designed apartments, each one crafted to balance luxury and practicality. As you approach Complex Base, its sleek exterior catches the eye, with large glass windows, smooth concrete walls, and subtle metal accents creating a harmonious yet bold look.

Saint Clair St. is a charming, intimate apartment building that houses six families, designed to feel like a small, close-knit community within a modern, urban setting. The building stands three stories tall, with a warm, inviting brick and wood façade that blends seamlessly with the neighborhood’s character. Its architecture has a classic touch, featuring large windows with wooden frames and elegant balconies that give each apartment a touch of individuality and outdoor space.
